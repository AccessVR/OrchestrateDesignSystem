**Card** — the brand's surface container: white on a tint section, `5px` radius, generous fluid padding, soft layered shadow. Roomy, not dense.

```jsx
<Card>Quiet resting card.</Card>
<Card elevation="crisp">Featured — signature offset shadow.</Card>
<Card bordered elevation="none">Flat hairline panel.</Card>
```

`elevation`: `card` (default soft) / `popover` (deeper) / `crisp` (rare playful offset) / `none`. `bordered`, `padded`.
